About Us

At Global Groove Radio, we're driven by your curiosity for ever-changing global music. We believe that music is a powerful force that makes us committed to keeping our listeners at the forefront of every new wave and trend.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an immersive internet radio experience, sharing the joy of diverse music discussions while creating a positive and laughter social media community. offering entertainment, engagement, and a platform for music enthusiasts to connect.

Our Vision

Our aim is to be the go-to internet radio destination, bringing joyful conversation and elevating happiness, discovery, and a shared love of music to the forefront of social media networks.
